# DES-004 — Donor Chair Measurement Protocol

**Organization:** Crohnoz Labs  
**Program:** HCL — Hardware Core Lab  
**Product:** HS-001 HydroSeat  
**Version:** 0.1.0  
**Status:** Draft  
**Purpose:** Capture the geometry and interfaces of the donor gaming chair before any destructive modification.

## 1. Required tools

- measuring tape;
- steel ruler;
- caliper if available;
- square;
- marker;
- masking tape;
- phone camera;
- scale if available;
- notebook;
- M6/M8 thread gauge or sample bolts if available.

## 2. Photography set

Take photographs before disassembly:

1. front;
2. rear;
3. left side;
4. right side;
5. top;
6. underside;
7. mechanism close-up;
8. armrest interfaces;
9. backrest interface;
10. damage and deformation details.

Every photograph shall include a ruler or known-size reference where practical.

## 6. Structural observations

Document:

- seat base material;
- steel plate condition;
- visible welds;
- cracks;
- corrosion;
- foam type;
- upholstery type;
- regions safe to cut;
- regions that must remain untouched;
- estimated load path;
- evidence of prior repair.

## 7. Destructive-work prohibition

Do not cut, drill or remove structural material until:

- measurements are completed;
- photographs are stored;
- the simplified CAD interface model exists;
- the planned cutout has been reviewed;
- the remaining load path is assessed.

## 8. Deliverables

- completed measurement table;
- photo folder;
- underside sketch;
- bolt-pattern sketch;
- simplified Fusion model;
- donor-chair interface drawing;
- list of uncertainties.
