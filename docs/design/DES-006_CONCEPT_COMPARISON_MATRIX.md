# DES-006 — Concept Comparison Matrix

**Product:** HS-001 HydroSeat  
**Version:** 0.1.0  
**Status:** Draft  

## 1. Concepts

### Concept A — Integrated replacement seat

Entire donor-chair seat replaced by a dedicated HydroSeat module.

### Concept B — Universal retrofit cassette

HydroSeat module attaches to multiple chair platforms through an adapter.

### Concept C — Independent therapeutic chair

Complete chair designed specifically for HydroSeat.

## 3. Working hypothesis

For prototype P0/P1, Concept A is expected to be the fastest route because the donor chair can provide:

- gas lift;
- wheelbase;
- backrest;
- armrests;
- tilt mechanism.

For the final commercial product, Concept C may offer the best control over safety, load rating, cleanability and regulatory evidence.

## 4. Decision rule

No concept is selected solely by lowest cost. Structural safety, hygiene and risk-control effectiveness have veto power.

## 5. Required evidence

- donor-chair measurements;
- simplified CAD;
- load-path sketch;
- basin-removal mock-up;
- cleaning workflow;
- preliminary risk comparison.
