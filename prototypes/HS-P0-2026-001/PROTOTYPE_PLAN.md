# HS-P0-2026-001 — Dry Geometry Prototype Plan

**Prototype class:** BENCH ONLY / DRY GEOMETRY  
**Human use:** Not authorized until dry-load review  
**Water:** Prohibited  
**Electricity:** Prohibited  

## Objective

Validate the seat outline, central opening, support zones and basin volume using inexpensive materials before detailed CAD or hydraulic integration.

## Proposed materials

- cardboard;
- foam scraps;
- MDF or plywood template;
- masking tape;
- removable container used only as a volume proxy;
- modelling clay or soft foam for contour studies.

## Prototype stages

1. full-size cardboard plan view;
2. central-opening cutout variants;
3. foam support-ring mock-up;
4. rigid base template;
5. basin-volume proxy;
6. dry sit test only after structural review.

## Variants

- P0-A: 220 × 280 mm opening;
- P0-B: 250 × 310 mm opening;
- P0-C: 280 × 340 mm opening.

Opening corners shall use generous radii. Sharp internal corners are prohibited.

## Measurements to record

- support comfort;
- thigh pressure;
- pelvic stability;
- central clearance;
- ability to stand;
- required seat height;
- basin clearance;
- interference with chair mechanism.

## Exit criteria

- one opening geometry selected for CAD exploration;
- no body weight carried by basin proxy;
- stable support zones identified;
- donor-chair interface remains feasible;
- all observations documented.
