# RMF-002 — Preliminary Hazard Analysis

**Product:** HS-001 HydroSeat  
**Version:** 0.1.0  
**Status:** Draft  

| ID | Hazardous sequence | Possible harm | Initial controls to investigate |
|---|---|---|---|
| HAZ-001 | Temperature-control failure exposes user to hot water. | Burn or tissue injury | Independent cutoff, limits, alarm |
| HAZ-002 | Water enters the electrical zone. | Shock or burn | Low voltage, isolation, enclosure |
| HAZ-003 | Frame or fastener fails under load. | Fall and trauma | Safety factor and load testing |
| HAZ-004 | Product tips during transfer. | Fall and injury | Stability envelope and anti-tip design |
| HAZ-005 | Excessive hydrojet pressure reaches sensitive tissue. | Pain, bleeding, tissue injury | Pressure limit and diffuse nozzle |
| HAZ-006 | Accessible intake creates suction. | Pinch or tissue injury | Guard and low-suction geometry |
| HAZ-007 | Incomplete cleaning leaves contamination. | Infection or irritation | Removable wet path and validated cleaning |
| HAZ-008 | Retained water promotes biofilm. | Infection | Drainability, drying and inspection |
| HAZ-009 | Material releases harmful constituents. | Irritation or sensitization | Material control and biological evaluation |
| HAZ-010 | Basin shifts or detaches. | Spill, fall or injury | Retention and installation indication |
| HAZ-011 | Overfill and displacement cause overflow. | Slip, electrical hazard or damage | Fill limit and overflow path |
| HAZ-012 | Seat height or geometry impairs transfer. | Fall | Transfer ergonomics |
| HAZ-013 | Opening concentrates pressure. | Pain or skin injury | Pressure mapping and adaptable interface |
| HAZ-014 | User exceeds session duration. | Skin damage or dizziness | Timer, IFU and warnings |
| HAZ-015 | User has impaired thermal sensation. | Burn | Contraindication and supervision |
| HAZ-016 | Product restarts after outage. | Unexpected heat or flow | Manual restart |
| HAZ-017 | Incompatible cleaning chemical is used. | Residue, irritation or failure | Approved-agent list |
| HAZ-018 | Product is shared without validated reprocessing. | Cross-infection | Single-user claim or validated reprocessing |
| HAZ-019 | Child accesses or climbs on the device. | Fall, burn or immersion hazard | Lockout, warnings and design |
| HAZ-020 | Incorrect service part is installed. | Loss of safety function | Controlled service parts |
